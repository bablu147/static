Privacy Policy for Reel Time Meter

Effective Date: January 3, 2025

Thank you for using "Reel Time Meter." We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data you provide. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data. Since "Reel Time Meter" is an offline tool, your data stays entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

"Reel Time Meter" does not collect any personal information from you. The app operates entirely offline, meaning:

  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.
  • All data related to your reel-watching time (such as time spent, number of reels, etc.) is stored locally on your device and not shared with any external entity.

2. How We Use Data

Since all data is stored on your device, we do not use your data for any external purposes. The app is designed solely to help you track your reel-watching time on your device and does not send or receive any information from the internet.

3. No Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services in "Reel Time Meter" that would collect or share any information about you. The app is 100% offline, with no internet access required for its core functionality.

4. Security

As no data is transmitted online, the security of your data is maintained solely through the security features provided by your device. We recommend using your device's built-in security measures, such as device passwords, biometric authentication, or screen lock, to protect your data.

5. Children's Privacy

"Reel Time Meter" is suitable for children and does not collect or share any personal information. The app is designed to be safe for use by all ages, including children, and operates entirely offline. Parents and guardians can feel confident that no data is being collected or transmitted from the app.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy in the future, the changes will be reflected in this document. As the app is offline, no external notifications or communications will be sent to you regarding policy changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the app, feel free to contact us at: support@reeltimemeter.com
